Future Vision: Affective Computing
The end goal is to evolve this V1 classifier into a real-time digital wellness dashboard. By monitoring facial micro-expressions in the background, we aim to provide users with data-driven insights into their weekly emotional trends, helping them identify peak productivity hours and emotional burnout triggers—all while maintaining a strict 'privacy-first' local-processing architecture.
